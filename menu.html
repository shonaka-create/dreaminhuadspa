    FAQ

    Booking & pricing

    What's included, what isn't, and how to choose between the treatments.

    Which treatment should I choose?

    If it is your first visit and you are due for a cut, take Head Spa & Cut — it is our signature service and the one most guests book. If your cut is fine and you are here for the scalp, Head Spa & Dry gives you the full ritual and the blow-dry finish without the cut.

    Not sure? Call us before you book and we will match the treatment to what your hair and scalp actually need.

    Is the blow-dry really included, or is it an extra?

    Included, always — on every head spa service, at no extra charge. The finish is part of the treatment, not an add-on. You leave styled and ready, never with wet hair.

    Every price shown is in Australian dollars and includes GST. There are no surprises at the counter.

    What is the Deep Treatment add-on, and do I need it?

    It is an intensive hair and scalp mask that adds 15–20 minutes and $30 to any service. It is worth it if your hair is dry, chemically treated or heat-damaged, and it can be decided on the day — after the scalp diagnosis, once we can see what your hair is asking for.

    How do I book, and what if I need to change my appointment?

    Book online in under a minute using the Book Now button, or send us a message. We work by appointment only, Tuesday to Saturday, 9:00am–6:00pm.

    As each booking reserves the room and your practitioner exclusively, we ask for at least 24 hours' notice for changes or cancellations.

    Do you sell gift vouchers?

    Yes. e-Gift Vouchers are available for any amount, delivered digitally through Square, and can be redeemed on any service. They are our most popular gift in the weeks before Father’s Day and Christmas.
