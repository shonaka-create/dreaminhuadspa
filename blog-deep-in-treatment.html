What is the Deep in Treatment?

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 5 min read

Healthy hair needs more than just a great conditioner—it needs the right environment for professional treatments to work at their best.

That's why we created the Dreamin’ Deep in Treatment, an exclusive add-on service designed to enhance your Japanese Head Spa experience and give your hair an extra level of care.

Using gentle warm mist technology followed by a cooling phase, this treatment helps maximise the effectiveness of your professional hair treatment, leaving your hair softer, smoother, and easier to manage.

What Is the Deep in Treatment?

The Dreamin’ Deep in Treatment is an optional upgrade that can be added to your Japanese Head Spa service.

After a personalised hair treatment has been applied, a specialised treatment cap is placed over your hair and connected directly to our professional mist machine.

The cap fills with warm micro-mist, surrounding every strand of hair with gentle heat and moisture.

This creates the ideal environment for your professional treatment to perform at its best.

How Does It Work?

The Deep in Treatment consists of two carefully controlled stages.

Step 1: Warm Mist Therapy (8 Minutes)

For approximately 8 minutes, warm micro-mist circulates continuously inside the treatment cap.

The gentle warmth helps soften the hair cuticle and allows the treatment to spread evenly throughout the hair while supporting better absorption of conditioning ingredients.

The humid environment also prevents the treatment from drying on the hair, allowing it to continue working throughout the process.

Step 2: Cooling Therapy (2 Minutes)

The treatment finishes with approximately 2 minutes of cooling.

As the hair cools, the cuticle naturally lies flatter, helping the hair feel smoother while enhancing softness and shine.

This warming and cooling process helps maximise the overall benefits of the treatment.

Who Is the Deep in Treatment Recommended For?

This treatment is especially beneficial for clients with:

  • Blonde or bleached hair
  • Colour-treated hair
  • Dry hair
  • Chemically treated hair
  • Heat-damaged hair
  • Hair that feels rough or porous
  • Frizzy or difficult-to-manage hair

If your hair regularly undergoes colouring, bleaching, heat styling, or chemical services, the Deep in Treatment provides additional nourishment and hydration.

Benefits of the Deep in Treatment

Adding this treatment to your Head Spa may help:

  • Improve hydration
  • Leave hair feeling softer and smoother
  • Enhance shine
  • Improve manageability
  • Reduce dryness
  • Support healthier-looking hair
  • Help professional treatments work more effectively

Many clients notice their hair feels noticeably silkier, lighter, and easier to style after the treatment.

Why Heat and Cooling Work Together

Professional hair treatments perform best under the right conditions.

The warm mist helps soften the cuticle and supports even distribution of the treatment throughout the hair.

The cooling phase helps smooth the cuticle afterwards, leaving the hair looking shinier while helping maintain moisture.

Together, these two stages create the perfect finishing touch to your professional treatment.

The Perfect Upgrade to Your Japanese Head Spa

The Dreamin’ Deep in Treatment is ideal for anyone wanting to give their hair extra care beyond a standard treatment.

Whether your hair is dry, damaged, colour-treated, or simply in need of extra hydration, this upgrade provides intensive professional care while you relax during your Head Spa experience.

Healthy Hair Starts with a Healthy Scalp

At Dreamin’, we believe beautiful hair begins with a healthy scalp—but maintaining the hair itself is just as important.

The Deep in Treatment combines personalised professional treatments with advanced mist technology to help your hair feel healthier, smoother, shinier, and easier to manage.

Every treatment is tailored to your individual hair condition, allowing us to provide the care your hair truly needs.

Because at Dreamin’, healthy hair begins with a healthy scalp, and beautiful hair is maintained with professional care.
