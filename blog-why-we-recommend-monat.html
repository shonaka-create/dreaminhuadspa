Why we recommend MONAT

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 5 min read

At Dreamin’, one of the questions we're asked most often is:

"Why do you recommend MONAT?"

With countless hair care brands available today—from supermarket shelves to luxury salon products—we understand why people ask.

The answer is simple.

We don't recommend MONAT because we sell it. We recommend it because we genuinely believe in its quality, ingredient transparency, and customer-first philosophy.

As head spa and scalp care professionals, every recommendation we make is based on education, experience, and real-world results—not marketing.

Healthy Hair Starts with a Healthy Scalp

At Dreamin’, we believe beautiful hair begins with a healthy scalp.

Every day we work with clients experiencing concerns such as:

  • Dry scalp
  • Oily scalp
  • Hair thinning
  • Damaged hair
  • Sensitive scalp
  • Age-related changes in hair quality

Because every scalp is different, choosing the right products is essential.

MONAT has developed a premium hair care range designed to support both scalp health and healthier-looking hair.

We would never claim that MONAT is the only great hair care brand on the market. There are many excellent products available.

However, after using the products ourselves and recommending them to our clients, we confidently believe that MONAT consistently delivers the level of quality we expect from professional salon hair care.

Ingredient Transparency You Can Trust

One of the biggest reasons we recommend MONAT is its commitment to ingredient transparency.

Knowing exactly what you're putting on your scalp and hair is important.

MONAT openly shares the ingredients used in its products, allowing customers to make informed decisions based on their individual needs and preferences.

For us, transparency creates trust—and trust is the foundation of every recommendation we make.

A 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Choosing a new hair care routine can feel overwhelming.

Everyone's hair is different, and what works for one person may not be perfect for another.

That's why we appreciate MONAT's 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee (subject to the official return policy).

It allows customers to try the products with confidence, knowing that if they're not completely satisfied, they have peace of mind.

To us, offering a guarantee reflects a company's confidence in its products and its commitment to customer satisfaction.

We Only Recommend What We Personally Use

Authenticity matters.

We would never recommend products that we wouldn't use ourselves.

Our team personally uses MONAT products, giving us firsthand experience and allowing us to recommend routines based on each client's unique scalp and hair condition.

Every recommendation is made with your long-term hair health in mind.

Great Products Need the Right Technique

Even the highest-quality shampoo won't achieve its full potential if it's used incorrectly.

That's why our support goes beyond simply recommending products.

We educate our clients on:

Proper shampoo techniques

  • Scalp massage
  • Healthy scalp habits
  • At-home scalp care
  • Choosing products based on individual scalp types
  • Education is just as important as the products themselves.

When you understand your scalp, you make better decisions for your hair.

More Than Hair Care

At Dreamin’, we're not simply selling shampoo and conditioner.

We're helping people build healthier scalps, stronger hair, and greater confidence.

That's why we only recommend products that meet our professional standards.

If you have questions about which products are right for you, we're always happy to help.

There is never any pressure to purchase.

Our goal is simply to help you find the best solution for your unique hair and scalp.

Start Your Healthy Hair Journey

If you're ready to experience healthier hair and a healthier scalp, we'd love to help.

Whether you're dealing with dry scalp, oily scalp, hair thinning, damaged hair, or you're simply looking for premium salon-quality hair care, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
