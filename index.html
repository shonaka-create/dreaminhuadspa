FAQ

Before your first visit

The questions we are asked most often. If yours isn't here, call us — we are glad to talk it through before you book.

What is a head spa, and how is it different from a hair wash?

A wash cleans the hair. A head spa treats the scalp — the skin your hair grows from. We begin with a consultation and scalp diagnosis, lift away the build-up that ordinary shampooing leaves behind, then deliver nutrients and moisture back into the scalp and hair through layered massage.

Dreamin' is a wet-type head spa: warm water throughout, and every session ends with a full blow-dry — you leave styled, never damp.

How long does it take, and what happens on the day?

Between 90 and 135 minutes, depending on the treatment. There are no shortened “express” menus — the ritual sets the pace, not the clock.

Your visit runs in four parts: consultation and scalp diagnosis, the head spa itself, a full blow-dry, then aftercare advice based on what we found on the day. Arrive five minutes early; there is nothing to bring and nothing to prepare.

Is a head spa right for me?

It suits most people, and it is especially worth it if you have an itchy or flaky scalp, roots that turn oily quickly, hair that has lost volume or shine, or a head that simply carries a lot of tension.

Because no two scalps are alike, products and methods are chosen for your condition on the day — never from a fixed routine. If you have a particular concern, mention it when you book and we will allow extra time in your consultation.

How often should I come?

Many guests return every four to six weeks, which keeps the scalp in condition between visits and pairs naturally with a cut. If you are working on a specific concern, a closer run of sessions at the start can make a visible difference sooner.

You will get an honest recommendation after your scalp diagnosis — along with the aftercare advice to hold the results at home.

Can I have a cut in the same appointment?

Yes — Head Spa & Cut is our signature service and the one most guests book. It combines the full head spa ritual with a precision cut and blow-dry finish in a single 120–135 minute visit.

Cuts are also available on their own, and a Deep Treatment can be added to any service.
