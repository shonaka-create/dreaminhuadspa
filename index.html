What is a Head Spa?

A healthy scalp grows glowing hair

A head spa is far more than a hair wash. It keeps the scalp in healthy condition — the foundation from which strong, glowing hair grows.

Type 01

Dry Head Spa

Performed without water, aimed purely at relaxation. Pleasant — but the scalp itself is left untreated.

Type 02 — The Dreamin’ Method

Wet Head Spa

Deep relaxation and true treatment care: cleansing away build-up, then delivering nutrients and moisture to hair and scalp.

Dreamin’ is a wet-type head spa.

IMAGE

Inside the Dreamin’ Method

Why our wet head spa works: a combination of crafts

The “treatment care” above comes from technique. We layer methods passed down through the Japanese beauty industry with the traditions of Indian head massage — first lifting away impurities, then supplying the nutrients your hair and scalp need.

And because no two scalps are alike, products and methods are chosen for your condition on the day — never from a fixed routine — in a completely private suite.
