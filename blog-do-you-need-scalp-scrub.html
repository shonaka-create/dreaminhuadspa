Do you really need a scalp scrub?

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 6 min read

Most people shampoo their hair several times a week.

So why would you ever need a scalp scrub?

Isn't shampoo enough?

The answer depends on what's sitting on your scalp.

At Dreamin’, we often meet clients who say:

"I wash my hair every day, but my scalp still feels oily."

"My hair feels heavy even after shampooing."

"My shampoo doesn't lather like it used to."

In many cases, the problem isn't that they aren't washing their hair.

It's that buildup has accumulated over time, and regular shampoo alone may not completely remove it.

That's where a scalp scrub can make a real difference.

What Is Scalp Buildup?

Every day your scalp naturally produces:

  • Sebum (natural oil)
  • Sweat
  • Dead skin cells

These combine with everyday products such as:

  • Styling creams
  • Hair sprays
  • Dry shampoo
  • Hair oils
  • Silicone-based products
  • Environmental pollution
  • Hard water mineral deposits

Over time, these layers can build up on the scalp.

Even if your hair looks clean, your scalp may not feel fresh.

Why Isn't Shampoo Always Enough?

Shampoo is designed to cleanse your scalp, but heavy buildup can sometimes be difficult to remove with shampoo alone.

Think of it like washing a frying pan.

If you cook with it every day, a quick rinse won't remove every layer of residue.

Occasionally, it needs a deeper clean.

Your scalp is no different.

A scalp scrub is designed to gently lift away stubborn buildup while refreshing the scalp.

What Makes the MONAT Scalp Purifying Scrub Different?

Not all scalp scrubs are created equal.

The MONAT Scalp Purifying Scrub combines several technologies to gently cleanse without leaving the scalp feeling stripped or overly dry.

Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple Cider Vinegar is naturally acidic and helps support the scalp's healthy pH.

Sugar Crystals

Unlike harsh exfoliants, the sugar crystals gently massage the scalp while gradually dissolving with water.

This helps remove buildup without aggressively scratching the skin.

Pink Clay

Pink clay helps absorb excess oil and surface impurities, leaving the scalp feeling cleaner and lighter.

MONAT's signature botanical oil blend helps maintain moisture, so your hair feels soft and comfortable after cleansing rather than dry or tight.

Together, these ingredients create what we like to call a weekly scalp reset.

Who Can Benefit from a Scalp Scrub?

A scalp scrub may be especially beneficial if you:

  • Have an oily scalp
  • Use styling products every day
  • Frequently use dry shampoo
  • Live in a hard water area
  • Feel like your hair becomes greasy quickly
  • Notice product buildup
  • Want your scalp to feel cleaner and fresher

Many people notice that after removing buildup, their shampoo lathers better, their hair feels lighter, and their conditioning products perform more effectively.

How Often Should You Use It?

More isn't always better.

For most people, using a scalp scrub once a week is enough to maintain a clean, balanced scalp.

If your scalp is particularly oily or you regularly use styling products, you may benefit from using it up to twice a week, following the manufacturer's directions.

The goal isn't to scrub harder.

The goal is to remove buildup gently while respecting your scalp's natural barrier.

A Clean Scalp Is the Foundation of Healthy Hair

At Dreamin’, we often say:

Healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp.

When buildup is removed, your scalp feels fresher, your hair feels lighter, and your daily hair care routine often becomes more effective.

A scalp scrub isn't something you need every day.

But when used correctly, it can become one of the most valuable tools for maintaining long-term scalp health.
