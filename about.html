Our Story

About Dreamin’

Japanese-quality technique, brought to the Gold Coast — one guest at a time.

Philosophy

The art of doing one thing, completely

Dreamin’ was founded on a simple conviction: a head spa should never be an add-on. It deserves the same devotion as any fine craft — unhurried time, a private space, and a practitioner who has spent a career mastering it.

Every appointment is a minimum of ninety minutes, always finished with a full blow-dry. Nothing rushed, nothing left to chance.

The Artisan

Twenty years of Japanese craft

Founder Takashi Iwata trained in the hair industry in Japan and has spent more than twenty years behind the chair. He brings trichology-informed scalp diagnosis together with the layered massage techniques of the Japanese head spa tradition.
