Our Philosophy

Why we chose the Takara Belmont YUME

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 6 min read

When people visit Dreamin’ for the first time, they often ask:

"Why does your shampoo feel so different?"

The answer isn't just in our techniques.

It's also in the equipment we choose.

At Dreamin’, we believe that if we're offering an authentic Japanese Head Spa, we should use equipment designed and developed in Japan by one of the world's most respected salon manufacturers.

That's why we chose the Takara Belmont YUME Shampoo System.

It isn't simply a shampoo chair—it's an essential part of creating the ultimate Japanese Head Spa experience.

What Is the YUME Shampoo System?

The YUME Shampoo System is a premium shampoo and head spa unit developed by Takara Belmont, one of Japan's most respected salon equipment manufacturers.

Designed specifically for comfort, relaxation, and professional scalp care, the YUME system allows clients to lie in a fully supported position while receiving shampoo and head spa treatments.

Unlike traditional shampoo basins, the YUME system transforms shampooing into a relaxing experience that feels closer to a spa treatment than a salon service.

Why Did Dreamin’ Choose YUME?

For us, choosing YUME wasn't simply about buying premium equipment.

It was about staying true to our philosophy.

If we're calling ourselves a Japanese Head Spa, we believe the experience should be authentically Japanese—from our techniques to the equipment we use.

Every detail matters.

Our goal isn't just to wash your hair.

Our goal is to create an experience that helps you relax, recharge, and enjoy the highest level of scalp care possible.

The Japanese Spirit of Omotenashi

In Japan, there is a concept known as Omotenashi.

Omotenashi means providing genuine hospitality with attention to every detail, anticipating a guest's needs before they even have to ask.

It's not simply about good service.

It's about creating an experience where the guest feels completely comfortable, respected, and cared for.

At Dreamin’, this philosophy guides everything we do.

Choosing the YUME Shampoo System is one way we honour that tradition by providing the most comfortable shampoo experience possible.

Comfort Makes a Difference

Anyone who has experienced neck discomfort at a traditional shampoo basin knows how difficult it can be to fully relax.

The YUME Shampoo System is designed to support the entire body, allowing your neck, shoulders, and back to rest comfortably throughout the treatment.

Many clients tell us they become so relaxed that they almost fall asleep.

When your body is comfortable, it's much easier for your mind to relax as well.

Why Takara Belmont?

Takara Belmont has been creating professional salon equipment in Japan for over 100 years.

The company is trusted by leading salons and luxury spas around the world because of its commitment to:

  • Exceptional craftsmanship
  • Japanese innovation
  • Comfort-focused design
  • Professional quality
  • Continuous research and development

Rather than simply manufacturing salon furniture, Takara Belmont designs equipment that enhances both the client experience and the stylist's ability to provide exceptional care.

Their products have become the global standard for premium salon experiences.

More Than a Shampoo Chair

At Dreamin’, shampooing isn't something we rush through before a haircut.

We believe shampoo is an important treatment in itself.

It's an opportunity to:

  • Cleanse the scalp properly
  • Relax the body and mind
  • Improve comfort
  • Support healthy hair
  • Create a moment of peace in a busy day

The right equipment allows us to deliver this experience exactly as it was intended.

A True Japanese Head Spa Experience

Many salons now offer "head spa" services, but authentic Japanese Head Spa is about much more than a scalp massage.

It's a combination of:

  • Professional scalp knowledge
  • Japanese techniques
  • High-quality equipment
  • Attention to detail
  • Genuine hospitality
  • A commitment to client comfort

Choosing the YUME Shampoo System reflects our commitment to delivering an experience that stays true to Japanese standards.

Why Details Matter

At Dreamin’, we believe that excellence comes from paying attention to the smallest details.

The products we use.

The techniques we perform.

The technology we invest in.

The comfort we provide.

Every decision is made with one purpose—to give our clients the very best Japanese Head Spa experience possible.

Because when every detail is thoughtfully considered, even something as simple as a shampoo can become an unforgettable experience.

Experience the Dreamin’ Difference

When you visit Dreamin’, you're not simply booking a shampoo or a scalp treatment.

You're experiencing Japanese craftsmanship, professional scalp care, and the spirit of Omotenashi working together.

That's why we chose the Takara Belmont YUME Shampoo System.

Because we believe our clients deserve more than a shampoo.

They deserve an experience.
