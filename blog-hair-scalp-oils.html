Why hair and scalp oils matter

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 6 min read

When people think about hair care, they often focus on shampoo and conditioner.

While these products are essential, there’s another step that is often overlooked—using a quality hair and scalp oil.

At Dreamin’ Head Spa & Sculpture, we believe healthy-looking hair starts with a healthy scalp, and the right oil can be a valuable part of both your professional salon treatments and your home-care routine.

Why Does Your Scalp Need Oil?

Your scalp naturally produces sebum, an oil that helps protect both the scalp and hair.

Sebum helps to:

  • Support the skin’s natural barrier
  • Reduce moisture loss
  • Keep the scalp feeling comfortable
  • Add natural softness and shine to the hair

However, factors such as frequent shampooing, colouring, heat styling, sun exposure, swimming, air conditioning, and seasonal weather can leave both the scalp and hair feeling dry.

The goal isn’t to make your scalp oily. It’s to help maintain a healthy balance.

Hair Needs Protection Too

Unlike your scalp, your hair is no longer living tissue.

Once the hair shaft has been damaged by heat, colouring, bleaching, or daily wear, it cannot repair itself.

This is why protecting the hair is just as important as cleansing it.

A quality hair oil can help:

  • Improve softness
  • Reduce the appearance of frizz
  • Enhance shine
  • Improve manageability
  • Reduce friction during brushing
  • Help protect dry or damaged ends

While oils cannot permanently repair damaged hair, they can improve the condition and appearance of the hair while helping reduce further mechanical damage.

No single product can transform your hair overnight.

Healthy-looking hair is the result of consistent care.
