What is marbb?

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 6 min read

When people visit a Japanese Head Spa for the first time, one of the questions we hear most often is:

"What is marbb, and why does it make my hair feel so different?"

The answer lies in its innovative nano bubble technology.

At Dreamin’, we believe that healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp, and marbb is one of the most effective tools available to create a cleaner, healthier foundation for both.

Let's explore what marbb is, how it works, and why so many salons across Japan have made it an essential part of their treatments.

What Is marbb?

marbb is a professional nano bubble system developed in Japan that combines only water and air to produce millions of ultra-fine bubbles.

These bubbles are much smaller than a strand of hair, allowing them to reach areas that ordinary water cannot, including the spaces between the hair cuticles and the tiny openings around hair follicles.

Unlike traditional cleansing methods, marbb doesn't rely on chemicals to enhance its performance—just advanced nano bubble technology.

How Does marbb Work?

The ultra-fine bubbles created by marbb carry a negative electrical charge.

As water flows through the hair and scalp, these bubbles attract and lift away positively charged impurities such as:

  • Excess sebum (oil)
  • Shampoo residue
  • Styling product buildup
  • Dirt and environmental pollutants

Because the bubbles are so small, they can cleanse areas that are difficult to reach with regular water alone.

Benefits of marbb for Your Scalp

A cleaner scalp creates a healthier environment for healthy-looking hair.

Using marbb may help:

  • Remove excess oil more effectively
  • Reduce product buildup
  • Cleanse around hair follicles
  • Leave the scalp feeling fresher and lighter
  • Improve the overall shampoo experience

A balanced scalp is the foundation of healthy hair growth.

Benefits of marbb for Your Hair

The benefits extend beyond the scalp.

Because marbb helps remove buildup without leaving excessive moisture inside the hair, many clients notice:

  • Softer hair
  • Increased shine
  • Better movement
  • Improved volume
  • Hair that feels lighter and cleaner

Many people say their hair feels cleaner for longer after a marbb treatment.

Can marbb Improve Colour and Treatments?

One of marbb's unique advantages is that it helps prepare the hair before colouring, perming, or treatments.

By removing excess residue and avoiding unnecessary water saturation, the hair is in a better condition to receive professional products.

According to the manufacturer, marbb can support more even penetration of colour, treatments, and perm solutions, helping improve the overall finish of salon services.

Faster Drying Time

Another noticeable benefit is reduced drying time.

Because marbb doesn't leave the hair holding unnecessary water, hair can dry more quickly after shampooing.

The manufacturer reports drying time may be reduced by up to 35%, which means:

  • Less time using a hair dryer
  • Reduced heat exposure
  • Less potential heat-related stress on the hair

This is particularly beneficial for clients who regularly style their hair using heat tools.

Is marbb Safe?

Yes.

One of the biggest advantages of marbb is its simplicity.

It uses only water and air—no additional chemicals, detergents, or additives.

This makes it suitable for most scalp and hair types, including people with sensitive scalps.

Who Can Benefit from marbb?

marbb is suitable for almost everyone, but it may be especially beneficial if you have:

  • Oily scalp
  • Product buildup
  • Fine or limp hair
  • Colour-treated hair
  • Permed hair
  • Dry or dull-looking hair
  • Frequent heat styling
  • Regular head spa treatments

It can also be incorporated into almost every salon service, including shampooing, colouring, treatments, perms, and Japanese Head Spa experiences.

Why We Use marbb at Dreamin’

At Dreamin’, our philosophy is simple:

Healthy hair begins with a healthy scalp.

marbb helps us create the cleanest possible foundation before every treatment by removing excess oil and buildup while preparing the hair and scalp for professional care.

Rather than masking problems, we believe in creating an environment where your scalp and hair can naturally look and feel their best.

That's why marbb is included as part of our commitment to delivering an authentic Japanese Head Spa experience.

Healthy Hair Begins with Healthy Water

The products you use are important—but so is the water used during your treatment.

By combining the power of water and air, marbb helps cleanse the scalp more effectively, support healthier-looking hair, and enhance the overall salon experience.

At Dreamin’, we combine advanced Japanese technology with professional scalp care to help every client achieve healthier, stronger, and more beautiful hair.

Because beautiful hair doesn't start with styling—it starts with a clean, healthy scalp.
