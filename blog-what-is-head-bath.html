What is head bath (頭浸浴)?

By Takashi Iwata Owner & Head Spa Practitioner 6 min read

One of the most relaxing experiences in a Japanese Head Spa is Head Bath (頭浸浴 / Tōshinyoku).

As warm water gently flows over your scalp while your head is immersed in a comforting stream, many people describe it as an instant feeling of calm and relaxation.

However, Head Bath is much more than a luxurious experience—it also offers practical benefits for your scalp and hair.

At Dreamin’, we believe understanding the purpose of Head Bath helps you appreciate why it has become one of Japan's most popular salon treatments.

What Is Head Bath (頭浸浴)?

Head Bath, or Tōshinyoku, literally means "head immersion."

During the treatment, warm water continuously flows over the forehead and scalp while the back of the head rests comfortably in warm water.

Unlike a traditional shampoo, the water flows continuously, creating a gentle waterfall effect that surrounds the scalp.

The experience combines warmth, water, and soothing sound to create deep relaxation while cleansing the scalp.

Why Do People Receive a Head Bath?

Head Bath isn't simply about washing the hair.

Its purpose is to:

  • Help relax the mind and body
  • Gently remove shampoo residue
  • Wash away excess sebum and buildup
  • Improve comfort during scalp treatments
  • Enhance the overall Head Spa experience

Many clients say it feels similar to soaking in a warm bath—but for your head.

Benefits of Head Bath

Deep Relaxation

The continuous flow of warm water creates a calming sensation that helps many people unwind after a busy day.

Combined with gentle scalp massage, it promotes a feeling of complete relaxation.

Cleanses the Scalp

The flowing water helps rinse away:

  • Shampoo residue
  • Excess oil
  • Loose dead skin cells
  • Surface impurities

Leaving the scalp feeling refreshed and clean.

Supports a Healthy Scalp Environment

Keeping the scalp clean and balanced helps create the ideal environment for healthy-looking hair.

Enhances the Head Spa Experience

Head Bath is often the highlight of a Japanese Head Spa because it combines cleansing with a deeply relaxing sensory experience.

Is Head Bath the Same as marbb?

No.

Although both treatments involve water, they serve different purposes.

marbb uses millions of ultra-fine nano bubbles to deeply cleanse the scalp and hair by helping lift away oil, buildup, and impurities.

Head Bath focuses more on relaxation while gently rinsing the scalp with a continuous flow of warm water.

At Dreamin’, these two technologies complement each other beautifully.

marbb provides an advanced deep cleanse, while Head Bath provides deep relaxation and a soothing finish.

What Is the Difference Between Head Bath and the Gold Halo Head Spa?

Many people have seen videos on social media featuring a gold semi-circular ring that pours water around the head.

Although they look similar, they are not exactly the same treatment.

The gold halo is primarily a visual water-flow device designed to create a relaxing waterfall effect around the head.

It produces stunning videos and offers a calming sensory experience.

Head Bath (頭浸浴), on the other hand, is a complete treatment concept developed in Japan.

It focuses not only on the flowing water but also on:

  • Controlled water temperature
  • Continuous circulation
  • Scalp cleansing
  • Relaxation
  • Integration into professional Japanese Head Spa treatments

While both create a peaceful experience, Head Bath is designed as part of a therapeutic scalp care service rather than simply providing a visual water feature.

Who Can Benefit from Head Bath?

Head Bath is suitable for almost everyone and may be especially beneficial for people who:

  • Feel stressed or fatigued
  • Experience scalp buildup
  • Have an oily scalp
  • Want a deeper sense of relaxation
  • Enjoy Japanese Head Spa treatments
  • Want to enhance their salon experience

Why We Include Head Bath at Dreamin’

At Dreamin’, we believe a Japanese Head Spa should care for both the scalp and the mind.

Head Bath allows our clients to slow down, relax, and enjoy a unique treatment while supporting a clean and healthy scalp.

Combined with professional scalp assessment, massage techniques, and advanced technologies such as marbb, it creates a truly authentic Japanese Head Spa experience.

More Than Just Relaxation

Head Bath is much more than a beautiful treatment you see on social media.

It's an experience that combines relaxation, gentle scalp cleansing, and Japanese wellness philosophy into one treatment.

At Dreamin’, every Head Bath is designed to help you leave not only with cleaner hair—but with a calmer mind and a healthier scalp.

Because true hair health begins with caring for the whole person, not just the hair.
